Business Sales Performance Analytics Dashboard

Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing business sales data to identify key trends, top-performing products, and regional performance. The goal is to derive meaningful insights and support data-driven decision-making.

 
 Objective

* Analyze sales and profit performance
* Identify top-selling products and categories
* Understand regional and segment-wise sales distribution
* Detect trends and patterns over time

 Tools & Technologies Used

* Power BI
* Data Visualization Techniques
* Basic Data Cleaning

 Key Insights

* The Consumer segment contributes the highest share of total sales
* Chairs and Phones are among the top-selling sub-categories
* Sales show a declining trend over time, indicating potential business concerns
* Sales are concentrated in a few states like California and Washington

 Recommendations

* Focus on expanding sales in low-performing regions
* Increase marketing efforts to boost declining sales trends
* Optimize pricing strategies to improve profit margins
* Prioritize inventory for high-performing products
* Explore growth opportunities in the corporate segment

 Dashboard Features

* KPI Cards (Total Sales, Total Profit)
* Sales Trend Analysis
* Sales Distribution by Segment
* Sales by Sub-Category
* Regional Performance Analysis
* Interactive Filters (Region Slicer)


 Outcome

* Gained practical experience in analyzing real-world sales data
* Developed skills in Power BI dashboard creation and data visualization
* Learned how to extract meaningful business insights from raw data
* Improved understanding of KPI analysis and decision-making strategies
